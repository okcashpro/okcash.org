  • Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does OK staking work in the new multichain system? Do I need to leave my wallet open to earn rewards?


    Staking OK on multichain is simple and doesn’t require you to keep a personal wallet open. Through secure staking pools on multiple networks like Base, BSC, Polygon, Avalanche, and Arbitrum, users can easily stake and earn rewards without running a node. Just connect your wallet, select your preferred chain, and start staking through Team.Finance's secure, audited platform. Rewards are managed by the pool directly, adding convenience and enhanced security.



  • How do I back up my wallet, and what should I do if I need to restore it?


    To back up your MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or similar wallets, securely save your 12- or 24-word recovery phrase. This phrase is your master key, allowing you to access your OK balance across any supported chain and device. Write it down on paper and store it offline, in a secure location, such as a safe, to protect against loss or unauthorized access.

    To restore your wallet, simply download the wallet app on a new device, select the "Import Wallet" option, and enter your recovery phrase. This will restore your OK balance and settings. Remember, do not share your recovery phrase with anyone, as it grants full access to your funds.



  • How long does it take for staked OK to earn rewards on the different chains?


    When you stake OK on any supported chain, your rewards are determined by the specific APY of that chain’s pool (Base, BSC, Polygon, etc.). These staking pools offer varying APYs based on total staked OK and market dynamics, providing an accessible way to earn rewards without specific wait times. After staking, rewards start accumulating immediately based on the pool’s terms.



  • What’s the best way to keep my OK secure across multiple chains?


    OK transactions across chains are secured through Team.Finance’s audited contracts and the decentralized networks they operate on. Always use a reputable wallet provider (like Trust Wallet, MetaMask, or Coinbase Wallet) with strong security features, such as encryption, to store your assets. Multisignature wallets are also recommended for added security, especially if handling large amounts.



  • What should I know about APY rates in OK’s multichain staking pools?


    Each OK staking pool on Base, BSC, Polygon, Avalanche, and Arbitrum has a unique APY based on the staking pool size, duration, and the total staked OK. For example, the APY may range from 82% on Base to 300% on Arbitrum. Check the specific pool’s APY before staking to select the option that best fits your reward goals.



  • Can I participate in OK’s governance through staking or holding OK?


    Yes! With the upcoming OK DAO, staking and holding OK across chains will enable participation in governance, including submitting and voting on proposals related to new projects, integrations, and strategic decisions. The goal is to create a fully decentralized governance model where the community has a direct impact on OK’s direction.
